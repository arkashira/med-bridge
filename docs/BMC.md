# Business Model Canvas – Med Bridge

| **Section** | **Details** |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Value Proposition** | • End‑to‑end encrypted transfer of medical documents.<br>• Multi‑factor authentication and HIPAA‑compliant storage.<br>• Real‑time status updates and a lightweight UI for clinicians and administrators.<br>• Reduces risk of data breaches and non‑compliance fines.<br>• Seamless integration with existing EHR systems via a simple API. |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **Hospitals & Clinics** – need secure, compliant document exchange.<br>2. **Private Practices** – small teams requiring low‑cost, HIPAA‑ready solutions.<br>3. **Telehealth Platforms** – secure patient record sharing.<br>4. **Health Insurers** – audit‑ready data transfer.<br>5. **Regulatory Bodies** – audit and compliance monitoring. |
| **Channels** | • Direct sales team (inside sales, account managers).<br>• Online marketplace for healthcare SaaS (e.g., HealthTech Hub).<br>• Partnerships with EHR vendors (Cerner, Epic).<br>• Conferences & webinars (Health IT, HIPAA compliance).<br>• Open‑source community (GitHub, PyPI) for trial and evangelism. |
| **Customer Relationships** | • **Self‑service**: SaaS portal with onboarding wizard.<br>• **Dedicated support**: 24/7 helpdesk for hospitals.<br>• **Account management**: Quarterly health‑check calls for large clients.<br>• **Community**: User forum, Slack channel, and open‑source contributions. |
| **Revenue Streams** | • **Subscription (SaaS)**: Tiered plans (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) based on number of users, storage, and API calls.<br>• **Per‑transfer fee**: Small fee for high‑volume or premium features (e.g., audit logs).<br>• **Implementation services**: Custom integration, data migration, and compliance consulting.<br>• **Marketplace add‑ons**: Integration plugins for popular EHRs. |
| **Key Resources** | • **Core codebase** (Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, S3‑compatible storage).<br>• **Security stack**: TLS, MFA, audit logging, encryption libraries.<br>• **Compliance certifications**: HIPAA, ISO 27001 (future).<br>• **Cloud infrastructure**: AWS/GCP/Azure with high‑availability and data residency options.<br>• **Team**: DevOps, security engineers, compliance officer, sales & support. |
| **Key Activities** | • **Product development**: Feature enhancements, bug fixes, UI/UX improvements.<br>• **Security & compliance**: Regular penetration tests, audit trails, policy updates.<br>• **Customer onboarding**: Setup, training, and data migration.<br>• **Marketing & sales**: Lead generation, demos, partner outreach.<br>• **Operations**: Monitoring, incident response, SLA management. |
| **Key Partners** | • **EHR vendors** (Cerner, Epic, Allscripts) – integration partners.<br>• **Cloud providers** – for secure, compliant hosting.<br>• **Compliance auditors** – to maintain HIPAA certification.<br>• **Healthcare IT consultants** – for implementation services.<br>• **Open‑source communities** – for code contributions and visibility. |
| **Cost Structure** | • **Infrastructure**: Cloud compute, storage, bandwidth.<br>• **Security & compliance**: Penetration testing, audit fees, certification maintenance.<br>• **Personnel**: Engineering, security, compliance, sales, support.<br>• **Marketing & sales**: Events, content, partner programs.<br>• **Legal & licensing**: HIPAA consulting, data‑privacy agreements. |
