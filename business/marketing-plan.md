# Marketing Plan for Med-Bridge

## Positioning
**Primary:** Secure and reliable platform for remote medical collaboration and data transfer.
**Alternatives:**
1. HIPAA-compliant virtual consultation and data-sharing hub for healthcare professionals.
2. Seamless, encrypted medical data transfer and collaboration for patient-centric care.
3. Trusted platform for secure remote consultations and medical data exchange.

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)
1. **Dr. Sarah Johnson**
   - **Daily Job:** Manages a busy internal medicine practice, coordinates with specialists, and handles patient data.
   - **Budget:** $5,000/month for software tools.

2. **Nurse Manager Lisa Chen**
   - **Daily Job:** Oversees nursing staff, coordinates patient care, and ensures data accuracy.
   - **Budget:** $3,000/month for operational tools.

3. **Hospital IT Director Mark Reynolds**
   - **Daily Job:** Ensures compliance, manages IT infrastructure, and secures patient data.
   - **Budget:** $10,000/month for security and compliance tools.

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $150 per acquisition.
   - **Target:** Healthcare professionals and IT managers.

2. **Google Ads**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $120 per acquisition.
   - **Target:** Searches for "secure medical data transfer" and "HIPAA-compliant collaboration tools."

3. **Healthcare Conferences and Webinars**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $200 per acquisition.
   - **Target:** Direct engagement with healthcare professionals and IT decision-makers.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Educational content on secure medical data transfer and remote collaboration.
- **Week 5-8:** Case studies and testimonials from early adopters.
- **Week 9-12:** Webinars and live Q&A sessions with healthcare experts.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize marketing materials and secure initial partnerships.
- **D-0:** Official launch with press release and social media blitz.
- **D+30:** First 100 users onboarded, initial feedback collected.
- **D+90:** Achieve 1,000 active users and $50,000 MRR.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** 200 by D+90.
- **$MRR Target:** $50,000 by D+90.
